About Nubia

Nubia Kelly Santos is an internationaly acclaimed dancer and led over 60 dancers at NBC Universal's Radio City event, performed with Mystic India – The World Tour, and was a Top 40 finalist on So You Think You Can Dance Australia.

Her signature energy, artistry, and feedback make her a sought-after instructor worldwide. With a unique blend of technical precision and emotional expression, Nubia creates transformative dance experiences.

Trained in multiple disciplines from classical ballet to Brazilian samba, Nubia's teaching philosophy centers on empowerment through movement, helping students discover their unique artistic voice.

Carnaval to Carnegie

Nubia has danced elite roles in Rio Carnaval and led over 300 international dancers through the parade. Her performances blend technical mastery with raw emotional power.

Nubia's unique background allows her to teach with anatomical precision while nurturing each dancer's artistic expression.
