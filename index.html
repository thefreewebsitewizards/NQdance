Your Questions

‚ùì Do I need prior experience?

Not at all! We offer classes for complete beginners through advanced professionals. Our foundational courses are designed to help absolute newcomers feel comfortable and make progress quickly, while our more advanced classes challenge experienced dancers to refine their technique.

‚ùì Are these live or recorded?

Both! You'll get access to our premium video library with hundreds of recorded classes across all levels and styles. We also offer live virtual classes multiple times per week where you can get real-time feedback from Nubia and connect with other students. Many students combine both for maximum flexibility and learning.

‚ùì What equipment do I need?

Just a device with internet access and enough space to move safely! For optimal experience, we recommend:

  • A computer/tablet with a large enough screen to see demonstrations
  • Comfortable clothing that allows movement
  • A yoga mat for floor work (optional)
  • Dance shoes or socks with grips for certain styles (we'll advise)

‚ùì Can I get personalized feedback?

Absolutely! In live classes, Nubia provides real-time corrections. For recorded classes, you can submit videos for critique through our exclusive student portal. Our private coaching sessions offer the most intensive personalized feedback, with custom choreography and training plans tailored to your goals.

‚ùì What's your cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation options because we understand life happens! For single classes, you can cancel up to 24 hours in advance for a full refund. Monthly memberships can be paused or canceled anytime before your next billing cycle. Private sessions require 48 hours notice for rescheduling or cancellation without charge.
